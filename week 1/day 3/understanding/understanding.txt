what is axis in numpy 

in 2D there are usually two axis !

x-axis: horizontal direction
y-axis: vertical direction


!!!!!!!
One important warning: NumPy’s axis=0 and axis=1 are programming conventions 
for array dimensions—not the same thing as the mathematical x-axis and y-axis



array.shape == (rows, columns)


axis=0 refers to the row dimension and operates downward across rows
axis=1 refers to the column dimension and operates across columns
